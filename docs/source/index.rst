New Narratives in Philosophy Digital Collection
===============================================

An open access, searchable database that will house and deliver a comprehensive 
bibliography of works by early modern women and well as digitized texts (as 
these become available), podcasts, and other resources that can be easily used 
by researchers, students, and teachers interested in including works by early 
modern women on their course syllabi.
